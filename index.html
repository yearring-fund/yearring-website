Security architecture

Designed for capital that matters.

Every layer of the protocol is built around protecting depositors first.

24-hour Timelock
All admin operations — including fee changes, strategy updates, and role grants — are queued on-chain and executable only after a mandatory 24-hour delay. No instant admin actions.
OpenZeppelin TimelockController · verified on Base
Emergency exit
In any crisis scenario, the guardian can immediately pause the protocol. Emergency exit mode allows all depositors to claim their pro-rata USDC share regardless of liquidity conditions.
Independent of Timelock · guardian-only
Reserve ratio
A configurable percentage of all deposits is kept as idle USDC in the vault at all times, ensuring standard redemptions can be served without waiting for strategy divestment.
On-chain parameter · visible to all
